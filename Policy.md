

# Privacy Policy for Qlerky: Password Manager

**Last updated:** 31.01.2026

Qlerky: Password Manager is a local-only password manager and two-factor authentication (2FA) app designed to keep your credentials secure and fully under your control. Your privacy is a core principle of this application.

---

## 1. Data Collection

Qlerky: Password Manager **does not collect any personal data**.

* No user accounts
* No email addresses
* No analytics
* No crash reporting
* No tracking technologies
* No identifiers of any kind

All data entered into the app remains on your device.

---

## 2. Data Storage & Security

All credentials and 2FA secrets are stored **locally on your device only**.

* Data is encrypted using a key derived from your master password
* The master password is **never stored**, transmitted, or shared
* The app works fully offline
* No servers or backend services are used

If you uninstall the app or lose your device, the stored data is permanently lost.

---

## 3. Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

If you store 2FA secrets (TOTP) in the app:

* Secrets are encrypted and stored locally
* One-time codes are generated entirely on your device
* No 2FA data is transmitted outside the device

---

## 4. Data Sharing

Qlerky: Password Manager **does not share data** with anyone.

* No third parties
* No advertisers
* No analytics providers
* No cloud services

Because no data is collected, there is nothing to share.

---

## 5. Internet Usage

Qlerky: Password Manager does **not require an internet connection**.

The app does not communicate with external servers or services.

---

## 6. Data Loss Disclaimer

If you forget your master password, **your data cannot be recovered**.

This is a deliberate security design to ensure that only you can access your stored credentials and 2FA information.
